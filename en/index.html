About Us

We are a Japanese Language School based in Sancti Spíritus (Cuba) since 2015. Instructing both students and workers in the culture and language of Japan.

What is our goal?

In 2017, with the arrival of Professor Akira Matsuo, many possibilities for self-improvement have opened up through learning Japanese in our province.

Given our knowledge, we have chosen to follow his legacy and continue teaching with his same willingness.

The Culture That Unites Us

Whether children, adults, or seniors, they are all hooked on Japanese tradition and culture.

No matter where you come from or who you are, its originality will not disappoint you!

And what an easy language to learn!

With such simplified structures and catchy phrases, it's impossible to get lost!
